About Community Aid Bridge

Community Aid Bridge is a platform that connects NGOs, volunteers and donors in one place. NGOs can post their requirements while volunteers and donors can easily find opportunities to help.

Our Features

NGOs

Post requirements and receive support.

Volunteers

Find nearby volunteering opportunities.

Donors

Donate food, clothes, books and funds.

Smart Matching

Connect resources with the right NGO.

How It Works

1️⃣ NGO Posts a Need

NGOs can post requirements like volunteers, food, books, clothes or funds.

2️⃣ Volunteers & Donors Join

People register as volunteers or donors and select how they want to help.

3️⃣ Smart Matching

The platform connects the right people with the right NGO quickly.
